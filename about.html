About Our School

Excellence in Technical Education Since 1991

Our History

Ejisu Senior High Technical School was established as one of the Community Day Schools in 1991. Over the years, we have grown into a premier technical institution in the Ashanti Region.

In recent times, we admitted boys into a temporary boarding house while construction of both the Girls and Boys Dormitory Blocks is ongoing.
