Admission Process

1

B.E.C.E Examination

Obtain entry forms from WAEC through your junior high school headteacher.

Computerized Selection

Ghana Education Service manages the computerized school placement system.

3

Check the school's notice board for the released admission list every September.

4

Registration

Successful candidates receive the school's official prospectus and complete registration.
